About the Himalayas

The Himalayas, stretching across India, Nepal, Bhutan, and Tibet, are home to the world’s tallest peaks, including Mount Everest. Famous for trekking, spiritual retreats, and breathtaking scenery, the Himalayas are a must-visit for adventurers and explorers alike.
