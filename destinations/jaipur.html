Jaipur, India

About Jaipur

Known as the "Pink City," Jaipur is the capital of Rajasthan and is famous for its grand palaces, majestic forts, colorful bazaars, and vibrant culture. A key stop on India’s Golden Triangle, Jaipur is a treasure trove of history and architecture.
